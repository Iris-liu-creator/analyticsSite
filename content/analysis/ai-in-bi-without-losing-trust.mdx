## The trust problem

AI can speed up analysis, but it can also blur the line between measured facts and generated interpretation. BI teams need clear guardrails before using AI in stakeholder-facing workflows.

## Where AI helps

AI is useful for first-draft summaries, anomaly prompts, query assistance, documentation, and stakeholder-specific explanations.

## Where AI needs limits

It should not silently redefine metrics, invent causal claims, or replace source-of-truth dashboards. Generated text needs traceability back to approved measures.

## Operating model

Use AI for draft and discovery steps. Keep certified datasets, semantic models, and human review as the control layer.
