## Executive dashboards are decision tools

An executive dashboard should answer three questions quickly: are we on track, where are we exposed, and what needs attention?

## Design principles

Lead with the business outcome. Use consistent comparison periods. Show exceptions clearly. Keep drill-down paths available without crowding the top-level page.

## Practical layout

Start with outcome KPIs, then trend, then segment breakdown, then risks or exceptions. Avoid making executives assemble the story from unrelated visuals.
