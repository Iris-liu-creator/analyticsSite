## Why GitHub-first works

For an early portfolio, GitHub-first content keeps the system simple. Posts and projects are versioned, reviewable, and easy to deploy.

## When to add a CMS

Add a CMS when editing becomes frequent, non-technical collaborators need access, or content workflows require drafts, approvals, and scheduling.

## A good migration path

Keep metadata consistent, isolate content loading helpers, and design components around stable content types. That way Supabase, Sanity, Contentful, or another system can replace file reads later.
