## Translation is the job

The most useful analysts translate constantly: from messy business language to measurable definitions, from data constraints to decision tradeoffs, and from dashboards to action.

## The dashboard is not the finish line

A dashboard is only successful if it changes how people understand performance or make decisions. Otherwise, it is just a well-arranged report.

## What I try to practice

Ask better questions, document definitions, show uncertainty clearly, and design every visual around the next conversation it should improve.
